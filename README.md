# Hospital-ER-Dashboard
Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard (Power BI) 🏥📊

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Emergency Room (ER) operations, patient flow, wait times, and service efficiency. This dashboard provides healthcare administrators with data-driven insights to improve patient care, reduce wait times, and optimize hospital resource utilization.

🚀 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to:

Analyze emergency room patient volume and trends

Monitor average wait time and service efficiency

Evaluate patient satisfaction and admission rates

Identify peak hours, high-load days, and patient demographics

Support operational decision-making and hospital performance improvement

🧮 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Total Patients: 9,216

Average Wait Time: 35.3 Minutes

Patient Satisfaction Score: 4.99 / 5

Total Patients Referred: 3,816

Admission Rate: 50.04%

Patients Seen Within 30 Minutes: 59.32%

📊 Dashboard Features
1. Consolidated View

Provides an overall summary of ER performance including:

Total patient count

Average wait time trend

Patient satisfaction score

Admission vs non-admission analysis

Patient distribution by age, gender, and race

Department referrals

Patient volume by day and hour (heatmap)

2. Monthly View

Enables detailed monthly performance analysis:

Monthly patient volume

Monthly wait time trends

Monthly satisfaction score

Monthly referral analysis

Patient demographics breakdown

Peak hours and busiest days

3. Patient Details View

Provides granular patient-level data including:

Patient ID and name

Gender and age

Admission date

Wait time

Department referral

Admission status

Patient race

This view helps in operational tracking and detailed analysis.

🎯 Business Use Case

This dashboard helps hospital management to:

Reduce emergency room wait times

Improve patient satisfaction

Optimize staff scheduling based on peak hours

Identify high-demand departments

Monitor admission trends

Improve overall ER efficiency and service quality

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

Microsoft Power BI – Dashboard Development & Visualization

DAX – Measures and Calculations

Excel / CSV – Dataset

Data Cleaning & Data Modeling – Power Query

Interactive Filters & Drill-through Features

📈 Key Insights Generated

Peak patient load occurs during specific hours and weekdays

Nearly 50% of patients require hospital admission

Majority of patients are handled within target wait time
